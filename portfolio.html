Multimedia

1. Electric Charge Stations Built in Chicago Since 2010 - Flourish Map

In crafting this digital map, I was able to learn how to incorporate data from the Chicago Data Portal, sift through that data on Google Sheets and then apply it to a map setting on Flourish. The map displays that ChargePoint Network created the most electronic charge stations of any EV Network.

2. Video: Students find community through residential colleges

This was the first video story I ever completed. I worked diligently with a fellow reporter, Jack Austin, to learn and use Adobe Premiere Pro all at the same time, ensuring that all specs related to audio and visuals were consistent with Newsroom style.


3. Average Employment Rate By Time From TAP Completion To Separation - DataWrapper

This graphic represents how the employment rate for new veterans differs depending on when those veterans chose to partake in a mandatory transition program prior to leaving the military. Those planning to leave the military who started the program sooner than their peers were more likely to be employed sooner after they left the military. This graphic relied on basic data provided by the National Directory of New Hires. Were I to go back, I would have more clearly labelled the horizontal access as "Time since departure from military" to clarify any confusion for viewers.

4. TAP Start Date For Active Duty Service Members Before They Leave The Military - DataWrapper

This graphic was a part of the same story as the above visualization. This visualization, pulled from data from the Government Accountability Office, depicts how many months prior to departing the military that active service members started a mandatory transition program. As evidenced by the data, almost 40% started the program less than six months before leaving the military. Additionally, though service members are required to start the program more than a year before departing the military, just about a quarter do so.

Methodologies

Electric Charge Stations Built in Chicago Since 2010 (Flourish) Data Breakdown

    1. Copied data from the Chicago Data Portal Highlighting Alternative Fuel Locations in the South Wisconsin, Chicago and West Indiana area.

    2. Through google sheets, created a new spreadsheet with just electric vehicle fuel types.

    3. Copied multiple data points (e.g. fuel type, latitude/lnogitute, EV Network) into a Flourish map focused just on the Chicago area.

Average Employment rate by time from TAP completion to separation (DataWrapper) Data Breakdown

    1. Data came from a report about the Transition Assistance Program (TAP) submitted to the Department of Labor.

    2. The data was pulled directly from page "xix" and translated into a line chart.

TAP Start Date For Active Duty Service Members Before They Leave The Military (DataWrapper) Data Breakdown

    1. Data was pulled from a graphic located in a study from the Government Accountability Office.

    2. I took data points from the study and translated them into the bar graphic form seen in the final product.
